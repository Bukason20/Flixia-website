About Flixia

Flixia company is a Nigeria streaming service company, founded in 2020. It offers film series and a collection of online games. Flixia can be accessed via web browsers or via application software installed on smart TVs, set-top boxes connected to televisions, tablet computers, smartphones, digital media players, video game consoles and virtual reality headsets on the list of Flixia-compatible devices. In January 2022, the company was ranked as the 5th among the top 10 best streaming services in the world. Flixia has it's headquarters in Awka, Anambra State.
